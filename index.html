Our Mission

Seeding Treasures

Seeding Treasures offers tailored lending programs and financial education courses to empower individuals to break the cycle of affordable housing dependency and build a more secure financial future.

We help low-income individuals who are currently receiving public assistance for affordable housing achieve greater financial stability by securing accessible lending options and providing financial literacy education.

Financial Independence

Providing resources to give people the ability to break free from the cycle of dependency on public assistance and gain control over their economic situation, making informed financial decisions, and being self-sufficient.

Community Participation

Achieving stable housing allows participants to engage and become a member of their community. Participation in local events, supporting neighbors, and contributing positively to the social fabric of their neighborhoods.

Educational Pursuits

Stable housing enables families to focus on education. Whether their own education or their children's with stable housing they can ensure regular attendance, completion of assignments, and involvement of their education.

What Seeding Treasures Offers

Comprehensive programs designed to build pathways to stable housing and financial well-being.

  • Innovative Housing Models
  • Streamlining Application Processes
  • Landlord Engagement Programs
  • Financial Literacy Education
  • Community Resource Centers
  • Supportive Services
  • Partnerships with Educational Institutions

The reliance on public assistance, but lack of traditional options leaves a cycle of dependency on public assistance programs. Outside of public assistance, there is shark lending, family, Habitat for Humanity, local community public programs, or charities. Seeding Treasures aspires to help participants achieve economic independence and homeownership by helping them face their significant barriers with lending programs that cater to their circumstance coupled with financial education which helps lead to a path of homeownership.

The value we offer is wrapped up in improved financial stability, increased financial literacy, increased savings, better credit, and ability to transition out of affordable housing.
