What Does My Donation Do?

We welcome all donations. Every dollar, hour, and expertise donated to Seeding Treasures is an investment in people's futures. Donating to Seeding Treasures is a powerful and compassionate way to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those facing housing insecurity.

Time

Expertise

Professional services to enhance our operational or programmatic effectiveness

Mentorship

Provide guidance and support to individuals striving for personal and professional growth
